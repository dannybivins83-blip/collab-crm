📋 Team Procedural Checklist

⚡ Speed to Lead

  • Respond to every new lead within 5 minutes — call first, then text + email.
  • Log the touch in the CRM and set the next follow-up date before moving on.
  • If no answer: leave a voicemail, send a text, and schedule a same-day retry.
  • Goal: appointment booked within 24 hours of the lead coming in.

📐 Inspection & Estimate

  • Karla enters the lead and creates the RoofGraf measurement report.
  • Upload the RoofGraf PDF to the lead → measurements auto-fill.
  • Build the estimate from the matching template; Apply measurements to fill quantities.
  • Review the estimate with the customer; send + log it. Set follow-up.

📝 Sign-Up & Documents

  • Signed proposal + deposit collected before scheduling anything.
  • Confirm color/material selection in writing.
  • Collect COI / scope docs; scan everything into the job's Documents.
  • Email signed copies to the customer; upload photos.

🏛️ HOA & Permit

  • If HOA: submit the application + color sample early — cannot pull the permit until approved.
  • Record the NOC; submit the permit package; pay fees; capture the permit #.
  • Post the permit at the job site and install the permit box.
  • Notify the customer of the start date once the permit is approved.

📦 Pre-Con, Material & Build

  • Order material; confirm delivery date; schedule crew + dumpster.
  • Pre-con walkthrough; install yard sign.
  • Tear-off → dry-in → dry-in inspection passed → install → magnet sweep.
  • Keep the customer updated at each milestone.

✅ Final & Close-Out

  • Pass final inspection; upload final photos.
  • Send final invoice; collect final payment per the draw schedule.
  • Issue warranty; request a review; ask for 3 referrals (names + numbers).
