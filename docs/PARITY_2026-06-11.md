## GAPS (ranked — these block financial parity)
1. **🔴 Job financials un-migrated.** 1,142 / 1,231 jobs — including all 662 closed + 477 invoiced — have **no `contract_value`**, and `collected`/`balance` roll up to ~$2.3k/$6.9k total. The AccuLynx `/financial-progress` collector (JobValue / PaymentsReceived / BalanceDue) did not run for the bulk of jobs. **Biggest parity blocker.**
2. **🔴 Payments not linked / not rolled up.** $1.008M across 178 payment records exists, but `invoices.paid_date` is empty on all 429 (0 paid) and `jobs.collected` sums to $2,290. The invoice↔payment linkage, paid-status, and job-level collected rollup didn't migrate.
3. **🟠 Photos not migrated** — 2 rows. Site/job photos need a collector pass (SiteCam + AccuLynx job photos).
4. **🟠 Documents partial** — 146 `library_docs`; ~900 files were known un-mirrored. Doc collector incomplete.
5. **🟡 Roof reports** — `roof_reports` table empty (they may live as measurements/library_docs PDFs; confirm representation).
6. **🟡 Comms** — no `comms` table; verify communications are captured in `activities` (7,869) and not lost.
7. **🔴 Serving-DB mismatch (local).** The app's configured DB is `data/crm.db` (**25 jobs**), not `crm_migration.db` (1,231). Locally the CRM serves a near-empty DB. Must confirm **Render is serving the migrated dataset**, not a stale `crm.db`.
